# Audio Settings - User Guide

## How to Control Audio in the App

### Accessing Settings

1. Open the app
2. Tap the **Settings** icon in the bottom navigation bar (far right)
3. You'll see the Settings screen with audio options

### Enabling/Disabling Word Pronunciation

#### To Turn Off Audio (Listen to Your Music):

1. Go to Settings
2. Toggle the **Word Pronunciation** switch to OFF
3. You'll see a message: "Audio disabled - your music will keep playing"
4. Now you can use the app while listening to Spotify or any other music app

#### To Turn On Audio (Hear Word Pronunciations):

1. Go to Settings
2. Toggle the **Word Pronunciation** switch to ON
3. You'll see a message: "Audio enabled - words will be pronounced"
4. During practice, words will be spoken aloud

### What Happens When Audio is Enabled

Even with audio enabled, the app is designed to be music-friendly:

- Your background music (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) will **continue playing**
- When a word is pronounced, your music volume will briefly lower
- After pronunciation, your music returns to normal volume
- This is called "audio ducking" and happens automatically

### What Happens When Audio is Disabled

- No word pronunciations are played
- Your music plays at full volume, uninterrupted
- All other app features work normally
- Perfect for quiet study or when listening to music

### Your Settings Are Saved

The app remembers your choice! You don't need to change the setting every time:

- If you turn audio off, it stays off when you restart the app
- If you turn audio on, it stays on when you restart the app

## Tips for Best Experience

### Studying with Music

- Turn audio OFF in Settings
- Play your favorite study playlist
- Use the app without any interruptions

### Studying with Pronunciations

- Turn audio ON in Settings
- The app will teach you how to say each word
- Your music will still play in the background (just quieter during pronunciation)

### Quick Access

- The Settings icon is always available in the bottom bar
- You can switch audio on/off anytime during your study session
- Changes take effect immediately

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My music stops when I open the app**
A: This shouldn't happen with the new update. Make sure you've updated to the latest version. If it
still happens, try disabling audio in Settings.

**Q: I can't hear word pronunciations**
A: Check that:

1. Audio is enabled in Settings
2. Your device volume is turned up
3. Your device is not in silent mode

**Q: The audio is too loud/quiet**
A: Use your device's volume buttons to adjust the TTS volume. The app respects your device's system
volume.

**Q: I want to hear pronunciations but not interrupt my music**
A: Keep audio enabled! The app will automatically lower (duck) your music temporarily when
pronouncing words, then restore it. This is the best of both worlds.
