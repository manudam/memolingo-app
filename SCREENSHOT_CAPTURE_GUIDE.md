# How to Capture App Screenshots for Google Play Store

This guide will walk you through capturing professional screenshots of your app for Play Store submission.

## 📋 What You Need

### Phone Screenshots (REQUIRED)
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 pixels (portrait)
- **Minimum**: 2 screenshots
- **Recommended**: 4-8 screenshots
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG

### Tablet Screenshots (OPTIONAL but Recommended)
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 1920 or 1920 x 1200 pixels
- **Minimum**: 2 screenshots (if providing)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG

## 🎯 What Screens to Capture

Capture screenshots showing your app's key features:

1. **Vocabulary List** - Main screen showing the vocabulary sets
2. **Practice Mode** - Flashcard or practice screen in action
3. **Word Details** - Detailed view of a word with definition, examples, synonyms
4. **Statistics** - Progress tracking and statistics screen
5. **Daily Streaks** - Achievement or streak tracking screen (optional)
6. **Settings** - Settings screen with audio options (optional)
