# Privacy Policy for 11+ Vocabulary Master

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025

## Introduction

11+ Vocabulary Master ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This
Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Automatically Collected Information

**Analytics Data (via Firebase Analytics):**

- App usage statistics (which features you use)
- Performance metrics (app crashes, loading times)
- Device information (device model, operating system version)
- General location (country/region, NOT precise location)

**Important:** We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information such as:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- Photos or media

### 2. Information You Provide

**Progress Data:**

- Vocabulary words marked as learned
- Practice session statistics
- Daily streak information
- Settings preferences

This data is stored ONLY on your device and is NOT transmitted to us or any third parties.

**Purchase Information:**

- In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play
- We do not collect or store payment information
- Purchase history is managed by Google Play on your device

## How We Use Information

We use the automatically collected analytics data to:

- Improve app performance and user experience
- Identify and fix bugs
- Understand which features are most useful
- Plan future updates and improvements

We do NOT use your information to:

- Show you advertisements
- Sell to third parties
- Create user profiles for marketing
- Track you across other apps or websites

## Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage:**

- All your vocabulary progress and settings are stored locally on your device
- This data never leaves your device
- You can delete this data at any time through your device settings

**Analytics Data:**

- Transmitted securely to Firebase (Google) servers
- Encrypted during transmission
- Anonymized and aggregated
- Cannot be used to identify individual users

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

**Firebase Analytics (Google):**

- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- Used for: Anonymous usage analytics
- Data shared: App usage patterns, device info, general location

**Google Play In-App Billing:**

- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Used for: Processing in-app purchases
- Data shared: Purchase transactions (handled entirely by Google)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students aged 8 and above preparing for 11+ exams. We do NOT:

- Collect personal information from children
- Require account creation
- Enable communication with other users
- Show advertisements
- Share data with third parties for marketing

Parents can review and delete all app data through device settings at any time.

## Your Rights and Choices

**Access and Deletion:**

- All your vocabulary progress is stored on your device
- You can view it within the app's statistics section
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings

**Analytics Opt-Out:**

- Analytics data collection can be limited through your device settings
- On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

**Purchase History:**

- Managed through your Google Play account
- Access via Google Play Store > Account > Purchase history

## Data Retention

**Local Data:**

- Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear data
- Not backed up to our servers

**Analytics Data:**

- Retained according to Firebase Analytics policies (typically 14 months)
- Automatically deleted after retention period

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## International Data Transfers

Analytics data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where
Google operates Firebase servers. These transfers are protected by:

- Encryption in transit
- Google's privacy and security standards
- Compliance with applicable data protection laws
